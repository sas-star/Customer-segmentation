Model Analysis

This chart compares three common clustering approaches across three critical axes:

  • Silhouette Score: Measures how well-defined and separated the clusters are (higher is better).
  • Scalability: Performance and memory efficiency when dealing with larger datasets.
  • Interpretability: How easily a human can explain or act upon the resulting segments.
KMeans is selected as the production model due to its high silhouette score and excellent interpretability.
